Happiest Birthday to the most beautiful soul 🫀🫂❤️

Thank you so much, beta, for everything you've done for me.

The first time I saw you standing near the shop with that innocent face, wearing a white and pink shirt, I instantly thought you looked really good.

One phone call completely changed our lives. I almost started liking you during that call itself—the way you talked, your patience, and how calmly you spoke to me that night.

The day we met, I liked you even more. I had told you many times about those unknown calls, and whenever one came, you would immediately ask who it was. That's where I saw your care and concern for me.

On January 1st, when you protected me, I developed a great opinion about you. I felt safe with you, like I was in good hands.

And the way you used to call me "Madam" always sounded so sweet and pleasant. It never failed to make me smile. 😊

We've faced so many problems together, but I don't want to include all of them here. Instead, I want to remember the beautiful moments, especially our temple date. The day you said "I miss you," those words made me emotional and stayed in my heart. 💕

All our late-night calls, conversations, and even our fights were special because they were with you.

When we spent almost 20 days together, I got to understand you completely—your love, care, attention, patience, and the way you always protected me. No matter how angry you were, you never showed it harshly towards me. Your patience and understanding meant so much.

Even our fights have now become beautiful memories that I'll cherish for a lifetime.

No matter how much I say, it will never be enough to describe how special you are to me. ✨

What I like most about you is your love and care. Whenever I say no, you stop. When I tell you something is wrong, you listen and try to understand. These are the things I truly admire about you.

But there are a few things I don't like as well. You get angry very quickly. Sometimes you find it hard to make decisions and keep things pending until the last moment, which often leads to confusion. You also get irritated easily at times. These are the things I wish you could improve.

Even with these flaws, your good qualities outweigh everything else. That's why you are so special to me. ❤️

Please try to control your anger a little and stop getting irritated so easily. Even if you raise your voice a little, I can't handle it—I end up crying. I know you don't like that, but when you're angry, it affects me deeply. That's the only thing I want you to change. Apart from that, you have so many wonderful qualities.

Sometimes I feel like you'll always stay with me, but at the same time, there's a fear that one day things might change. Still, I pray to God every day that you remain in my life. It's not selfishness—it's because you mean so much to me. If one day you feel happier with someone else, I would let you go where your heart truly belongs.

Thank you so much for being with me all these days. I sincerely wish that you stay in my life for a lifetime.

Happiest Birthday to my precious one... ❤️

No matter what happens, you will always be one of the best parts of my life, and I will always remember you.

From the bottom of my heart, I pray that you stay happy, achieve great things, reach even better heights in life, and live peacefully without any disturbances.

Once again, Happy Birthday to my charm. ❤️🩹👣...
